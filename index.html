About Varnave

Varnave is where tradition meets modern vibes — a two-day cultural spectacle packed with performances, competitions, food, and unforgettable moments. It’s the heartbeat of the campus, bringing together students, artists, and communities to celebrate diversity in the most vibrant way possible.

About JIT

Jansons Institute of Technology (JIT) is a premier engineering institution located in Coimbatore, India. Established in 2009, JIT has been at the forefront of technical education, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
